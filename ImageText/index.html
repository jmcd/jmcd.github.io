Image Text Support

How do I use the camera to get text?

Tap circular "shutter" button, and a photo will be taken. You can perform actions by selecting the photo from the bottom left.

How do I change the area of what's being analysed?

The crop area can be changed by dragging the triangular upper-left area of the area.

How does this work?

A trained machine learning algorithm analyses your image on device, and extracts pieces of text. Text is then glued together and data, such as phone numbers, are detected.
